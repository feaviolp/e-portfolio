Induction Module

An introduction to Artificial Intelligence and postgraduate study expectations.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Demonstrate the technical and personal requirements of online learning.
  2. Demonstrate competencies in the use of the tools available to online learners.
  3. Demonstrate academic skills, e.g. essay/report writing, research techniques, referencing, reflective learning and critical thinking at a level commensurate with Postgraduate study.

Artefacts

The mandatory artefact for this module was a 750 word essay (+10%) with the title "Discuss the importance of a postgraduate degree in the Computer Science field".

  • Read the essay (opens in a new tab)

In addition, this e-Portfolio was created in readiness for capturing reflections from this and all subsequent modules in the programme.

Reflections

The essay seemed relatively simple, which is unsurprising because it was ungraded and I believe its purpose was simply to ensure that students can operate effectively at postgraduate level rather than being overly focused on the content itself.

There was, however, some confusion over formatting which needs to be made clearer in future. I understood that essays are typically written without headings, but the tutor advised that we should use headings (fine by me - I find it much easier to structure a report or essay with headings). We were then told to include our name and word count in a header sheet, but then, after clarifying guidance on the VLE that said essays were marked blind, we were told to exclude the header sheet. Part of my feedback was that I didn't include the word count, which is fair enough, but it was all rather confusing when we made our final submissions about precisely what we should and should not include. I hope that future modules are much clearer so we can be confident in the presentation requirements so we can spend time focusing on the content.

Meeting Notes

Two Q&A sessions were held with the course tutor. I attended both in real time, accompanied by 2-3 other students, but most watched the recordings.

Following the activity to introduce ourselves in the discussion forum, a few students also created as WhatsApp group which we used to keep in touch and check understanding of the assignment requirements such as the need or not for a cover page.
